# Privacy Policy for SpendLeak                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                      
  **Last updated: April 22, 2026**                                                                                                    
   
  ## 1. Overview                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                    
  SpendLeak ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance tool that detects recurring subscription charges from your SMS inbox. We
   are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your rights.
                                                                                                                                      
  ## 2. Data We Access                                                                                                              

  SpendLeak requests permission to read your SMS inbox (`READ_SMS`) solely to identify recurring payment patterns. No SMS data is ever
   transmitted off your device.
                                                                                                                                      
  ## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect                                                                                                      

  - We do not upload, transmit, or share your SMS messages with any server, third party, or cloud service.                            
  - We do not collect your name, phone number, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  - We do not sell your data.                                                                                                         
  - We do not use your data for advertising.                
                                                                                                                                      
  ## 4. On-Device Processing Only
                                                                                                                                      
  All analysis is performed locally on your device using on-device machine learning. Your financial data never leaves your phone.

  ## 5. Analytics

  We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregate usage statistics (e.g. number of app opens, feature usage). This data     
  cannot be linked back to you and contains no SMS content or financial information.
                                                                                                                                      
  ## 6. Data Storage                                        

  App preferences (such as dismissed subscriptions and theme settings) are stored locally on your device using Android's standard     
  storage. This data is never backed up to external servers.
                                                                                                                                      
  ## 7. Permissions                                         

  | Permission | Purpose |
  |---|---|
  | `READ_SMS` | Read inbox to detect recurring charges |
  | `INTERNET` | Required by Firebase Analytics |
  | `VIBRATE` | Haptic feedback |                                                                                                     
   
  ## 8. Children's Privacy                                                                                                            
                                                            
  SpendLeak is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

  ## 9. Changes to This Policy                                                                                                        
   
  We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
